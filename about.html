What is CALL of DUTY:warzone?

Call of Duty: Warzone is a free-to-play battle royale game from Activision, known for its fast-paced action, massive maps, and unique features like the Gulag and custom loadouts. Released in 2020, it supports up to 150 players per match and offers cross-platform play. Warzone has become a global hit, combining intense gameplay with constant updates and strong ties to the wider Call of Duty franchise.
